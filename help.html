We're here for you!

HOW TO CREATE AN ACCOUNT

Go to the main page of the website then click on 'sign in' button. There you find a form to fill with the nickname you want, your email adress and then you must create a strong password. After that you can add a profile pic so your friends recognize you easier.

HOW TO TRACK YOUR READING

In your profile page you can find an 'new book' button to add a new book that you're reading or you can use one that you're already reading it, for that you have a 'manage books' button, that will show you the lists of your books, curently reading list of books or finished books. You go on to the book you want to track and press the 'manage' button, there you can select the chapter or even page that you're on and even add notes to that.

HOW TO SHARE WITH FRIENDS

You can share anything with your friends on Boo! After every event like adding a new book to reading list or a note on a chapter that you enjoyed you can share it by pressing the 'share' button on the screen, so that this event will appear on your friend's feed!
